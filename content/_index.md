I am a PhD candidate at the [Institute of Machine Learning](https://ml.inf.ethz.ch/), ETH Zurich, supervised by [Prof. Valentina Boeva](https://boevalab.inf.ethz.ch/). My research focuses on developing novel machine learning algorithms for spatial omics, with applications in cancer biomarker discovery and hypothesis generation.
{{< /lead >}}

My work sits at the intersection of **graph neural networks**, **self-supervised learning**, **spatial transcriptomics**, and **computational oncology**. I develop methods that leverage the spatial organization of cells in tissue to uncover structural patterns relevant to cancer biology.
